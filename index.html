Crystal Meth Collector

You wil be given a random number of kilos of crystal meth that you need to make at the start of the game.
There are four types of meth below. By clicking on a meth you will add a specific number of kilos to your total weight.
You win the game by matching kilos needed to the random number, you lose the game if your total weight goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game, the game will change the values of each crystal.
